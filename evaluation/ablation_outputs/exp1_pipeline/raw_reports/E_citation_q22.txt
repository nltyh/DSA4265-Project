**Bearish**  
The sentiment is predominantly bearish due to the Argentine government's pre-emergency declaration affecting gas supplies, which directly impacts crude oil and diesel markets by raising concerns over supply chain blockages.

### 2. KEY RISK SIGNALS

**Supply (Production/Upstream) | Supply Chain Blockage | Severity: High**
